## Design Context

### Users
High-end clientele, partners, and stakeholders interacting with the "One Heart" brand ecosystem. The context is professional, premium exploration where users evaluate the brand's network and services, expecting a flawless "quiet luxury" digital experience.

### Brand Personality
Quiet luxury, editorial, premium, deliberately crafted. It should evoke confidence, calmness, and sophistication without being overly aggressive or "loud."

### Aesthetic Direction
"Dark Luxe" editorial. 
- **Theme**: Dark mode, utilizing deep black backgrounds (#000, #0a0a0b) with soft pearl white accents (#f0eee9) and subtle highlighting (`var(--color-pearl-glow)`).
- **Typography**: `Clash Display` for bold, impactful headings paired with `Outfit` (and multilingual fallbacks) for refined, highly readable body copy.
- **Motion**: Cinematic and human-crafted. Rhythmic sync and deterministic interactions that bypass unreliable browser transitions, avoiding "bounce" for smooth, exponential decelerations. 
- **Anti-patterns to avoid**: Side-stripe alert borders, decorative gradient text, glowing glassmorphism over-usage, layout jank/shift.

### Design Principles
1. **Editorial Restraint**: Embrace negative space and asymmetric layouts. Not everything needs to be in a card. Keep content breathing.
2. **Physicality & Rhythm**: Enhance scrollytelling and transitions using smooth, scroll-driven "push" effects rather than abrupt opacity fades. Interactions should feel tactile.
3. **Inclusive Excellence**: True luxury means accessibility. Maintain viewport zoom, robust ARIA semantics, and screen-reader compatibility alongside high-end aesthetics.
4. **Deliberate Light & Shadow**: Use nuanced surface lighting (like pearl mist highlights) rather than generic drop shadows to create depth.
